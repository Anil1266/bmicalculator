About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple formula that uses your height and weight to estimate whether you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

BMI Range Category Health Risk
Below 18.5 Underweight Nutritional deficiency risk
18.5 – 22.9 Normal Low health risk
23 – 24.9 Overweight Moderate risk
25 and above Obese High risk of lifestyle diseases

Indian BMI standards are slightly stricter because health risks such as diabetes and heart disease may occur at lower BMI levels in Asian populations.
