💧 Water Intake Calculator

How to Use the Water Intake Calculator

This calculator estimates how much water you should drink each day based on your body weight.

Steps to Calculate Your Daily Water Intake

  • Enter your body weight in kilograms
  • Click the calculate button
  • The calculator will estimate your recommended daily water intake

Why Drinking Enough Water Is Important

Water plays a vital role in maintaining body temperature, supporting digestion, transporting nutrients, and removing waste from the body.

Benefits of Proper Hydration

  • Improves energy levels
  • Supports brain function
  • Helps regulate body temperature
  • Promotes healthy skin

You can also read our guide on daily hydration to learn more about staying properly hydrated.
