Healthy Weight Tips

1. Eat Balanced Meals

Include proteins, vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and healthy fats in your daily diet.

2. Exercise Regularly

Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water supports metabolism and overall health.

4. Get Proper Sleep

7–8 hours of sleep helps regulate hormones related to weight control.
