What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple health measurement that uses your weight and height to estimate whether your body weight is within a healthy range.

Formula: BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m²)

For example, if a person weighs 70 kg and is 1.7 meters tall:

70 / (1.7 × 1.7) = 24.22

BMI Categories for Indian Adults

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 – 22.9 – Normal
  • 23 – 24.9 – Overweight
  • 25 and above – Obese

BMI is widely used as a screening tool but does not directly measure body fat percentage.
