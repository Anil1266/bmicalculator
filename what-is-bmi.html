What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement that uses height and weight to estimate whether a person has a healthy body weight.

BMI Formula: Weight (kg) / Height (m²)

BMI is widely used as a screening tool to identify potential health risks.

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 – 22.9 – Normal
  • 23 – 24.9 – Overweight
  • 25+ – Obese
