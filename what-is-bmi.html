What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.

It is calculated by dividing weight in kilograms by height in meters squared.

BMI Categories

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 to 24.9 – Normal weight
  • 25 to 29.9 – Overweight
  • 30 and above – Obese

Maintaining a healthy BMI helps reduce the risk of heart disease, diabetes, and other health problems.
